About


Shauna James is a fashion and portrait photographer from Donegal who now lives and works in Dublin.


Starting her career, Shauna first picked up a camera at the early age of fourteen - influenced by self-portraiture and conceptual photography. Growing up with the rise of social media and digital techniques enabled Shauna to see the bigger picture of an international industry. After graduating college in Letterkenny Co. Donegal she made the move to Dublin, where she naturally felt the transition to fashion photography.


Shauna enjoys creating timeless and emotive imagery. She is inspired by feminine strength, unique beauty and lasting style.


She now hosts regular workshops and body positivity photography sessions in her studio in Malahide photographing members of the public in a raw and unretouched style to celebrate the diversity and variety of bodies that we don't always see in modern media.
